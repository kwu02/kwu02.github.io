WURFL Capabilities

Here are some capabilities offered by WURFL database that worth noting:

Name Usage
device_orientation Detect the device's orientation (portrait or landscape) to adjust content and layout accordingly.
is_touchscreen Identify touchscreen devices to offer touch-friendly interactions and navigation
is_mobile Detect if a device is a mobile phone. This capability can be used to determine whether to deliver a mobile-optimized version of a website or application.
